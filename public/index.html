Our Story

Designing Spaces That
Breathe Life

ArtSpace was born from a simple belief: that the spaces we inhabit shape who we become. Founded in 2012 in Nairobi, we set out to bring world-class interior design to East Africa — spaces that honour both international aesthetics and the rich warmth of African heritage.

Over the past decade, we've had the privilege of transforming over 150 homes, offices, and hospitality spaces. Each project is approached as a unique canvas, with no two designs alike.

Our philosophy is simple: listen deeply, design boldly, and deliver spaces that clients fall in love with — not just on handover day, but every single day thereafter.

Sophia Mwangi

Founder & Creative Director
